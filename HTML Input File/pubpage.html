This bibliography cites major publications that appeared during the calendar year and were written or edited by current and emeritus members of the Library faculty. "Major" publications include books, chapters in books, monographs, journal articles, and published conference proceedings, whether in print or electronic format. Book reviews, brief items in newsletters, committee reports, abstracts, and the like are not included.

Librarian for Library and Information Science and Research Support Services, Dan Tracy (dtracy@illinois.edu) currently maintains this bibliography. Please send additions and corrections to him.

Database design and programming were completed by Assistant University Archivist, Chris Prom, and until 2012 the database was maintained by the former Librarian for Library and Information Science, Sue Searing.

In the complete list, citations are arranged by year (latest first), then within year alphabetically by first author. This database was last modified May 29, 2019.
